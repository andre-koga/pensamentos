# it pays off

being tired tired me  
so i decided  
to try something new  
(don't get things wrong)  
although i'm smiling  
it still hurts  
but, you know  
deep down i realized:  
trying to fight  
trying to be happy  
trying for myself  
trying for others that i care  
...it isn't easy, but  
it pays off

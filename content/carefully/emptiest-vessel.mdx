# emptiest vessel

I'll be the emptiest vessel

the bravest warrior  
the funniest clown  
the most arrogant dictator  
the most awkward nerd  
the craziest party-goer  
the most philosophical dreamer

I'll forever be the emptiest vessel

Unable to act of my own volition  
As a puppet of the circumstances  
With a lot of potential but no jumpstart  
Covered by the shadows, just waiting

# Tunnel

Rage in my gut,

screams that go unheard.

"I'm not done yet!" I cry,

but the tunnel stretches on, endless.

No echoes, no response,

just the slow burn of fury.

But eventually the anger subsides,

and the first tear falls,

then a flood, a lake, a sea.

The tunnel finally has content.
